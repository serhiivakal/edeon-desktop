- **Intended Use**: Training and benchmarking Tier-1 QSAR Daphnia acute EC50 prediction models.

### `earthworm_acute_lc50`
- **Intended Use**: Training and benchmarking Tier-1 QSAR earthworm acute LC50 prediction models.
- **Known Biases**:

  - Significant bias towards legacy insecticide and herbicide chemical space.

### `fish_acute_lc50`
- **Intended Use**: Training and benchmarking Tier-1 QSAR fish acute LC50 prediction models.
- **Known Biases**:
  - Species-specific and chemical-specific bias in ECOTOX reporting (focus on standard test species like Rainbow Trout and Fathead Minnow).

### `rat_acute_oral_ld50`
- **Intended Use**: Training and benchmarking Tier-1 QSAR rat acute oral LD50 prediction models.
- **Known Biases**:
  - Over-represents industrial chemicals and legacy pesticide compounds relative to modern pesticide discovery chemistry.

### `skin_sensitization`
- **Intended Use**: Training and benchmarking Tier-1 classification QSAR skin sensitization models.
- **Known Biases**:
  - Strong bias toward fragrance chemicals, cosmetic ingredients, and common industrial preservatives.
  - Over-represents strong and non-sensitizers relative to weak sensitizers.
  - Binary classifications collapse potency ranges, masking the distinction between weak and strong sensitizers.

### `soil_dt50`
- **Intended Use**: Training and benchmarking heteroscedastic Tier-1 QSAR soil DT50 prediction models.
- **Known Biases**:
  - enviPath data primarily represents commercial pesticide active ingredients and key industrial chemicals subject to regulatory testing requirements.
  - Significant bias toward compound classes like triazines, organophosphates, and urea herbicides.
  - Soil type, organic matter, temperature, and moisture variations are not explicit features, contributing to high within-compound target variance.

### `soil_koc`
- **Intended Use**: Training and benchmarking Tier-1 QSAR Soil Koc prediction models.
- **Known Biases**:
  - OPERA dataset representation bias: dominated by industrial chemicals and standard organic pesticide classes.
